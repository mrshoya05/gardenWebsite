Company Garden Mussoorie

History of Company Garden

Company Garden was initially developed by an Englishman, Dr. H. Fackner, during the British colonial period in India. It was later renovated and renamed as Company Bagh after independence. The garden served as a recreational space for the British during their stay in Mussoorie.

Features:

The garden is beautifully landscaped and well-maintained, offering a peaceful retreat amid nature. It features a variety of flowers, shrubs, and trees, making it a colorful and scenic place to visit. There are numerous fountains, ponds, and walking paths within the garden.
